Games Workshop is the premier miniature wargaming company that created Warhammer 40k. Their local store is located in downtown Victoria and has supplies for your every Warhammer 40k need. The store also has tables that anyone can drop by and use. Their staff is incredibly friendly and welcoming to players of all skill levels. In addition to Warhammer 40k they also supply Games Workshop's other amazing miniature wargames: Warhammer Age of Sigmar and the Lord of the Rings.
Games Workshop is located at 625 Johnson St. Victoria.
Call at: 250 361-1499
