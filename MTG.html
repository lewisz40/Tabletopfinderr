Located on the 600 block of Johnson Street (Victoria's "comic book row"), Yellowjacket boasts the city's largest collection of Magic: the Gathering Singles. It is also home to the best attended weekly Magic: the Gathering Tournaments. Yellowjacket is also the homebase for the loadingreadyrun crew who make some of the best magic related vidoes. Magic games are played around the clock so you can drop by anytime and play. Yellowjacket also has magic tournements going on everyday so there is always something happening.
Yellowjacket is located at 649 Johnson St. Victoria.
Call at: 250 480-0049

Triple Play Sports & Games opened its doors in May 2014 in Esquimalt, British Columbia. Jason Sarrazin had his own iconic Field of Dreams moment … “If I build it, they will come” after retiring from 17 years in the Canadian Armed Forces. He wanted to reignite the spark of passion for collecting amongst sports fans, both young and old, and welcome players from Victoria’s thriving gaming community. Staff at Triple Play are ready to introduce young collectors to the joys of opening a fresh pack of sports cards, or to assist the seasoned collector in finding that perfect showcase item to take their collection to the next level. Triple Play also hosts a regular schedule of Magic: The Gathering and other gaming events for players looking to spend a few hours immersed in their favourite game with friends.
Triple Play Sports & Gaming Cards is located at 1209 Wharf St. Victoria
Call at: 250-384-3311
